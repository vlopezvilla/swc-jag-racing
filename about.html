About Us

Our Journey in Automotion

At Jag Racing, we're not just a team; we're a community of innovative minds and off-road enthusiasts. Driven by a passion for engineering excellence and a love for the thrill of the track, we break boundaries and redefine what's possible in the world of Baja SAE.

From designing cutting-edge off-road vehicles to conquering challenging terrains, our team embodies the spirit of adventure and the pursuit of victory. What sets us apart is our commitment to precision, collaboration, and pushing the limits of off-road engineering.

Join us on a journey from the classroom to the racetrack, where theory meets practice, and every turn is an opportunity to showcase our skills.

About Us

Jag Racing is a student led program proudly representing Southwestern Community College. We aim to put classroom knowledge into practice by designing, fabricating, testing, and competing in Baja SAE. Join us in our journey!
